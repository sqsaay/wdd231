Site Plan

Site Name:

Your guide to live music

Site purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide users with comprehensive information about live music concerts, including artist details, contact form of Join Us, and ticketing options. It aims to be a one-stop resource for music enthusiasts looking to stay updated on their favorite artists and upcoming events.

Scenarios:

Where can I join to this concerts' radar?

How can I know if they are on tour?

What are the best ways to purchase concert tickets?

Color schema:

Secondary colors: #f2a900 and #d63447. Will be used on the cards templates.

Tertiary colors: #004666 and #fefefe. Will be used on Main content.

Body font: Roboto and Quicksand (Google Fonts)
